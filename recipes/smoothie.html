How to Make a Healthy Smoothie

Description

The recipe involves using natural ingredients to create a really healthy and sweet tasting smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Blueberries
  • 1 cup of oat milk
  • 2 tbs of sunflower butter
  • 1/4 cup of chia seeds
  • 1 peeled banana

Steps to Cook

  1. Throw all ingredients in blender
  2. Blend for 1 minute
  3. Add some sunflower butter to taste
  4. Serve and enjoy!
