How to Make a Healthy Buffalo Tofu Wrap

Description

You will spend less than 5 minutes preparing this awesome buffalo tofu wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of hard firm tofu
  • 1 bottle of buffalo hot sauce
  • 1 package of spinach wraps
  • 1 head of lettuce

Steps to Cook

  1. Pan fry the tofu on a skillet for 5 minutes
  2. Season tofu to taste
  3. Remove tofu and mix with whatever amount of hot sauce you like
  4. Pack as much tofu into one toasted spinach wrap as humanly possible
  5. Chow that thing down!
