How to make delicious tostadas

What your delicious tostada will look like

The recipe entails using healthy goods to make the most succulent, mouth watering, spicy tostadas you have ever laid you eyes on.

Ingredients

  • Tostada shells
  • Morning start veggie chicken
  • Crushed red peppers
  • Lettuce
  • Guacamole

Steps to cook

  1. Cook morning start chicken in oven on 350F for 5 minutes
  2. Chop chicken into pieces when done
  3. Spread chicken on tostada
  4. Cover with lettuce
  5. Top it off with guacamole and crushed red peppers
  6. Serve and enjoy!
